Problem Solving Quiz

1. If a box contains 12 red balls and 18 blue balls, what is the probability of drawing a red ball at random?

2.A train travels from City A to City B at a speed of 60 mph and returns from City B to City A at a speed of 45 mph. If the distance between the cities is 240 miles, what is the average speed for the round trip?

3.A triangle has a base of 8 units and a height of 10 units. What is its area?

4.If a shirt originally costs $40 and is on sale for 25% off, how much will the shirt cost after the discount?

5. A bookstore has 150 books on its shelves. If 40% of the books are fiction, how many books are fiction?

6.Samantha wants to divide 60 candies equally among 5 friends. How many candies will each friend receive?

7.A rectangular garden is 18 meters long and 12 meters wide. If you want to increase its area by 50% while keeping the width constant, what should be the new length?

8.A car travels a distance of 360 miles in 6 hours. What is its speed in miles per hour?

9.If a backpack costs $80 after a 20% discount, what was its original price?

10.John can paint a room in 5 hours, while Sarah can paint the same room in 4 hours. How long will it take them to paint the room together?
